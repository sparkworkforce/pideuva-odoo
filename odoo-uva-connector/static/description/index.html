🛵 Uva PR Connector

Receive Uva orders in your POS & dispatch deliveries with Uva Fleet — all from Odoo.

Puerto Rico's local delivery platform, integrated natively with Odoo Enterprise.

Two Integration Flows

📥 Flow A — Incoming Orders

Uva customer orders appear automatically in your POS. Staff accept, reject, or mark items unavailable — without leaving the register.

  • Real-time popup with sound notification
  • Configurable auto-accept timer
  • Mark individual items as unavailable
  • Order queue for busy periods
  • Multi-store support

📦 Flow B — Uva Fleet Delivery

Dispatch deliveries via Uva Fleet drivers directly from sale orders or stock pickings.

  • Cost estimate before dispatch
  • Native delivery.carrier integration
  • Real-time status tracking via chatter
  • Cancel deliveries from Odoo
  • Automatic retry on transient failures
