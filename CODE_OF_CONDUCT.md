# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone involved in this project.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and constructive in all communications
- Accept feedback gracefully and provide it thoughtfully
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, and code reviews.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to info+fleet@pideuva.com. All reports will be reviewed and addressed appropriately.
